Best Chocolate chip cookie

This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe ever! Simple ingredients, quick, very easy to prepare and absolutly delicious. These chocolate chipcookies comes out perfectly every single time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter softend
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salts cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  3. Bake for about 10min in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned
